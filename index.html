About Andrea

Andrea Golden is a licensed REALTOR® with Oakwyn Realty Ltd. and an active member of the Real Estate Board of Greater Vancouver. A lifelong BC local, Andrea has lived in multiple Vancouver neighborhoods, giving her a personal understanding of the city's diverse communities and housing market.

Her professional journey is defined by adaptability and dedication. After starting her career in technology sales, she pivoted into real estate when she purchased her first property and discovered a passion for the process.
